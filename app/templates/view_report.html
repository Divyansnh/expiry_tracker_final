Inventory Health Metrics

These metrics help you understand the overall health of your inventory. They are calculated using industry-standard formulas and provide insights into your inventory management efficiency.

Stock Coverage

{{ "%.1f"|format(report.report_data.get('summary', {}).get('inventory_metrics', {}).get('inventory_health', {}).get('stock_coverage', 0)) }}%

Items with adequate stock

Calculation: (Items with quantity ≥ 10) / Total Items × 100

Importance: Indicates how well you're maintaining stock levels

Limitation: Doesn't consider optimal stock levels for different items

Expiry Ratio

{{ "%.1f"|format(report.report_data.get('summary', {}).get('inventory_metrics', {}).get('inventory_health', {}).get('expiry_ratio', 0)) }}%

Items expiring soon

Calculation: (Items expiring in 7 days) / Total Items × 100

Importance: Shows the proportion of inventory at risk of expiry

Limitation: Doesn't consider item values or quantities

Value at Risk

{{ "%.1f"|format(report.report_data.get('summary', {}).get('inventory_metrics', {}).get('inventory_health', {}).get('value_at_risk', 0)) }}%

Inventory value at risk

Calculation: (Value of items expiring in 7 days) / Total Value × 100

Importance: Shows the financial impact of expiring items

Limitation: Based on cost price, not potential sales value

Stock Turnover

£{{ "%.2f"|format(report.report_data.get('summary', {}).get('inventory_metrics', {}).get('stock_turnover', 0)) }}

Average value per item

Calculation: Total Inventory Value / Total Items

Importance: Indicates the average investment per item

Limitation: Doesn't reflect actual turnover rate without sales data
